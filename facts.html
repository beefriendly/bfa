A Few Bee Facts

Queen cell with larvae sitting in pool of royal jelly.

There are 3 types of Honey Bees (Apis Mellifera) in every colony. (Clockwise from lower left) Workers (female), Single Queen (female), and Drones (male). The most destructive pest in any hive of bees is the Varroa Mite. Picture credit-Alex Surcica
